Project Overview

The AutoRally platform is a high-performance testbed for advanced perception and control research. The robot, developed at Georgia Tech, is integrated with ROS and designed as a fully contained system that requires no external sensing or computing. The robot is robust, cost-effective, and safe platform that opens the space of high-speed, agressive autonomous off-road driving to all researchers.
