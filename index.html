Project Overview

The AutoRally platform is a high-performance testbed for advanced perception and control research developed at Georgia Tech. It is a self-contained system with no need for external sensing or computing. The robot allows researcher to explore high-speed, agressive off-road autnomous driving, which is a space typically open only to large groups with significant capital.
